GES : A School Management

GES, what is this?

This is a Web application that allows managements for schools.

Why creating an application like that?

Current technology is well advanced, yet the management of the majority of schools and institutions in Madagascar leaves something to be desired.
This is the main reason why we decided to design an application that can help administrators of any school.

Why using GES?

The GES app is designed to be easy to use . At the same time, it is complete .
It has several features that are useful to any institution.
These features will allow, for example, to manage the payment of school fees made by students. or the follow-up of absences and delays of students as well as professors.
